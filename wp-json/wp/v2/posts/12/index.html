The most recent iPhone 15 Pro Max boasts a 48-megapixel rear camera, three lenses, and an impressive 8064 x 6048 resolution.<\/p>\n

But does the newest and most expensive iPhone model necessarily have the best camera? The answer is not so straightforward.<\/p>\n

So, what iPhone has the best camera for you? The decision depends on various factors.<\/p>\n

For some users, waiting a few years between upgrades may be more sensible, as the difference in camera capabilities from one year to the next might not be substantial. However, if you’re using an older iPhone, the camera improvements in the latest model could be truly impressive.<\/p>\n
